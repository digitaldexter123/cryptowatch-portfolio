Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay?
We accept all major credit cards through our secure Stripe checkout. You can also use Apple Pay or Google Pay.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
What happens after the free trial?
You'll be automatically charged $5/month unless you cancel before the trial ends. We'll send you a reminder 3 days before your trial expires.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption for all your data and never store your crypto wallet keys or private keys.
